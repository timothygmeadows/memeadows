camp north end

this morning, melinda and i went to this area called Camp North End. We are at Hex Coffee, and the surrounding garden is so beautiful. She's doing her work at duke energy, and i'm just doing random work like writing this blog and also seeing if i can visit landdesign.

This is giving midtown at carmel. false sunflower takes over your eyes around the coffee shop. meandering pathways to get lost. the vibes are also Chelsea Market-esque. it is very industrial, and the planting are very very nice. the texture of the sumac trees are amazing. i want to take more photos to keep here, that's really important. but i wonder how those sumac trees do here.
